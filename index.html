Our Story

Hi I’m Mike, the proud owner of Callum's Delicatessen! Since 1923, my family has served generations of customers in the Chicago area. In the early part of the twentieth century, our neighborhood was home to millions of immigrant families. With the lack of public and private transportation, Callum's Delicatessen became a community and center point for congregating. On Sundays, the neighborhood frequented our deli to enjoy meals my family prepared with love. After a long week of work, we thought it best to make sure our customers’ bellies were full of delicious food so that they would have enough energy for the start of the work week.

Not only is the food amazing, but everyday we aim to put a smile on the faces of our patrons. Loved by celebrities and people from all walks of life, Callum's Delicatessen keeps our tradition alive by promising to always put our customers first for years to come.
